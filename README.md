Play a Touhou inspired "shoot-em-up" featuring classic Touhou characters and encounters.

This is a Touhou Minecraft fan mod. All rights to characters, world, and concepts belong to [Team Shanghai Alice](https://www16.big.or.jp/~zun/) and the original creator ZUN.

## Overview
Bullet Hell introduces arcade-style shoot-'em-up mechanics into Minecraft. Fight new bosses, avoid complex projectile patterns, and survive fast-paced combat scenarios focused on movement and positioning. This mod is designed for players who enjoy difficult encounters and improving their skill through repeated attempts.

## Features
* **Boss Battles:** *Fight unique bosses with multiple phases. Attacks become more complex as the fight progresses.*
* **Bullet Patterns:** *Dodge structured projectile waves inspired by classic bullet-hell games.*
* **Combat Arenas:** *Encounters take place in a dedicated 2D overlay arena where positioning and reaction time are key.*
* **Customisation:** *The system is designed to be expandable. Modpack creators and server owners can adapt bosses, patterns, and stages to fit their needs.*

## Getting Started
Type the command `/bullethell start` then select your stage, difficulty and character. Then learn the attack patterns.

Success depends on timing and movement. Expect to fail a few times while learning encounters.
